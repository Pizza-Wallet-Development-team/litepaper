The wallet as simple as pizza

    Purpose of the paper

    This paper provides an overview of Pizza wallet, a one-stop solution for anyone who’s just getting into Web3, where anyone can buy, store and transact their crypto with ease on multiple chains. This is not intended to be a deep technical white paper, but an introduction to Pizza wallet’s vision and a call to action to all collaborators, potential partners and users.

    Introduction

    It’s for you if you want all your crypto requirements met at a single place with the most user-friendly crypto experience out there, but it’s also for you if you’re not too familiar with the crypto space yet and want your crypto journey to be as fun as eating a slice of pizza.

    Around pizza, Neapolitans do not only feel comfortable but also get a very easy going vibe when approaching their warm, authentic and simple core food. Eating a pizza is lived as a true experience where ingredients from around the world, from the tradition and the contemporary meet together to give life to something in constant evolution. Pizza is like a metaphor of life, always transforming.

    This is why pizza is at the core of our development, as we want Pizza wallet’s user experience to be as fun, easy and family as eating a slice of pizza.

    On Pizza wallet, users are presented with the option to choose how they want to sign up - by email, familiar social ids or with a secure seed phrase.The ease of Web2 and the security of Web3. Best of both worlds, isn’t it?

    It’s not your regular wallet though. With Pizza wallet, you can do pretty much everything that you currently have to use multiple platforms for, such as exchanging your fiat for crypto and vice versa, making transactions on multiple chains, storing your crypto, etc. Why go through the struggle of finding multiple platforms when all of it can simply be done at one single place?

    We believe that crypto usage should be inclusive, accessible and easy to use by everyone and for that to happen, the onboarding and the transacting process has to be simplified which is exactly what we are aiming at Pizza wallet.

    Mission

    Making the crypto onboarding experience fun and enjoyable, just like eating a slice of pizza.

    Vision

    We are tapping into this huge community worldwide by integrating crypto use cases in one of the most popular and familiar things to eat - pizza - for onboarding the next 1 million people to Web3.

    Problem

    The first step towards getting into crypto or Web3 in general is creating a wallet. Though currently if some novice decides to do that, it leaves them confused and disappointed. Why? It appears to be a totally alien place with dozens of technical jargons that people don’t understand. In fact the process of signing up itself is too technical with so many security checkpoints.

    Once users have managed to set up a hot wallet, next they have to undergo a continuous decision making process of choosing among multiple platforms and multiple chains. And it doesn’t just end there. When you have to use one platform for buying crypto with your fiat and another for swapping it, and then yet another to store it or turn it back to fiat, everything starts to feel like a tedious task if you’re not already used to it. Not to mention that not every platform supports multiple chains. So since none of this is integrated well, it gets hard to navigate through even for those who’ve been doing this for a while.

    It would’ve still been better if only there were enough supportive communities to guide you through but that’s not the case either.

    All in all, crypto comes off as a complicated space made only for the nerds and even the nerds can find it complex sometimes. This notion is lethal to the growth of this space. If we’re striving for mass adoption of Web3 and crypto, the onboarding process has to change for the better.

    Roadmap

    Our vision is supported by the roadmap, which will be a journey of tech developments, Web3 education and user feedback.

    1

    Quarter 1 of 2023

    Release and test the MVP beta version

    We decided to build it in the form of a webpage application as browser extensions are more prone to being hacked. This brings a level of security for our users, which we believe is essential in their Web3 journey.

    2

    Quarter 2 of 2023

    NFT gallery

    Users will be able to see their NFTs across multiple chains without the hassle of having to use multiple wallets for that. Moreover, the NFT integration opens the door to multiple use cases like collectables, proof of attendance badges, subscription contracts, even property ownership contracts. The use cases are limitless and many yet to be explored.

    3

    Quarter 3 of 2023

    Launching the mobile app

    Most paid and financial apps are used from mobile, hence we aim to open up to a bigger market while meeting the users’ needs at the same time.

    4

    Quarter 4 of 2023

    DeFi integration with Compound and Aave

    DeFi integrations will empower the user to earn while holding crypto assets by staking their assets on some of the major protocols out there.

    5

    Quarter 1 of 2024

    Cashback in collaboration with major pizza brands

    Having the circular economy as one of our main drivers, we will let people be able to cash back while eating pizza at our partners’ shops.

    Long term vision

    Crypto as of now comes off as something made for the geeks only, not only because the technology is built by them, but also because the industry has made great progress in the last years, which make newbies have a difficult time joining. We are changing that.

    How?

    Education is a big component that is driving our product further and by introducing crypto use cases in a thing as regular yet special as pizza, we develop a user’s smooth transition from Web2 to Web3.

    Accessibility goes hand in hand with the educational side of things for mass adoption. It also comes shaped in tech integration to have a one stop shop where you have everything at your fingertips.

    Our long term vision is to have a worldwide impact in how Web3 adoption is being built, as well as partner up with the biggest players out there to offer an easiness of access to crypto or NFTs, be it transacting it or storing it.
